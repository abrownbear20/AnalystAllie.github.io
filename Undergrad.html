Blast Wave Propagation in a Brain Surrogate

This project was a comparative exploration of peak pressures in a simulated blast wave chamber with two brain surrogates of different material compositions. This research was conducted at Virginia Tech and sponsored by the NSF.

Fatigue Analysis of a Medical-grade Material Used in a Cardiac Device

This project served to locate possible failure modes of an implantible material used in a cardiac medical device developed to provide direct cardiac compression in the later stages of congestive heart failure.
